Only thing available for now.... riddles !

I haven't invented any of those. I just found them online and thought they were good ones.

I don't like riddles based on a play of words. I don't like riddles where, once you know the answer, you say "Oh but you didn't say that could be possible". I like riddles that are solved logically, where the logic is hard to find, and where you say "holy shit that's right" when given the answer (I can not guarantee the latest for every riddle though.)

The riddles are ranked by 'difficulty'.
